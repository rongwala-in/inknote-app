Privacy Policy for InkNote

Effective Date: January 2025

1. Information Collection

InkNote does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All notes, drawings, and documents are stored locally on your device only.

2. Data Storage

All content you create in InkNote (notes, drawings, imported images, PDFs) remains on your device. We do not have access to your data.

3. Third-Party Services

InkNote does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Permissions

InkNote may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save and load your notes locally
  • Camera/Photos: To import images into notes (optional)

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and data never leaves your device.

5. Children's Privacy

InkNote does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on your device using standard Android/iOS security mechanisms.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page.
